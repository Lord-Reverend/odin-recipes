Curry Stand Chicken Tikka Masala Sauce

Description

This is the greatest of all simple curry recipes. It freezes well if you are actually have some leftover. Add 2-4x more garlic if you are using pre-minced garlic. This recipe isn't particularly spicy, so feel free to add more cayenne pepper.

Keep in mind that dairy is included, but no other major alergens should be present in this recipe.

Ingredients

    • 2 tablespoons ghee
      1 onion, finely chopped
      4 cloves garlic, minced
      1 tablespoon ground cumin
      1 teaspoon salt
      1 teaspoon ground ginger
      1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
      1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
      1/4 teaspoon ground turmeric
      1 15 oz can tomato sauce
      1 cup heavy whipping cream
      1 tablespoon white sugar
      2 teaspoons paprika
      1 tablespoon vegetable oil
      2 skinless, boneless chicken breasts cut into bite-size pieces
      1/2 teaspoon curry powder
      sugar and salt to taste
